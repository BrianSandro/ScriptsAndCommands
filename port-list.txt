Dynamic, private or ephemeral ports
See also: Ephemeral port

The range 49152–65535 (215+214 to 216−1) contains dynamic or private ports that cannot be registered with IANA.[314] This range is used for private, or customized services or temporary purposes and for automatic allocation of ephemeral ports.
